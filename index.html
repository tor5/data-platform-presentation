A Fresh Start for Data

Multi-disciplinary teams

going after most valuable business questions

produce new measures, insight & recommendations

For Example

board: Can we improve the profit we generate from players?

team: OK, let's think about maximising wallet share of our current players

team: We need a new measure, let's estimate wallet size with demographics and wagering behaviour

team: Oh, we get 20% less share of wallet from mid-value members

team: Let's try this new promotion mechanic

board: Thanks, we've made 5% more from mid-value members this quarter, what's next?
